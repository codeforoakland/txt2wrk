Txt2wrk is an easier way to connect job seekers to employers.

Job seekers can listen to job postings and submit their resume to employers using any mobile phone.

  • I'm looking for work
    • Find out about new jobs as soon as they are posted, even without a computer.
    • Listen to job descriptions over the phone.
    • Apply to jobs immediately with any mobile phone.
  • I'm looking to hire
    • Get access to more applicants.
    • Reach out to people instantly wherever they are.
    • Get responses faster and get the help you need.
How it Works
  • Create a Profile

    Sign up with txt2wrk, register your phone, create a Job Seeker profile describing the kinds of work you are looking for, and optionally upload a resume.

  • Post Jobs

    Sign up with txt2wrk and post job openings for free.

  • Receive a TXT

    When employers post jobs matching your qualifications, you are immediately notified of the job via text message.

  • Listen & Apply to Jobs

    Call txt2wrk and listen to the job description.

    If you like what you hear, apply for the job right over the phone. Your contact information and Job Seeker profile are immediately sent directly to the employer.

  • Review Applications

    Job Seeker profiles are collected for each job posting as job seekers respond to your posting. Review individual profiles and contact prospective hires directly.

  • Receive Call Backs

    Employers who are interested in your profile will contact you directly on the phone you registered with txt2wrk.

About txt2wrk

Txt2wrk was awarded the Ramsell Prize for the best app at the Code for Oakland hackathon on June 4, 2011.

Txt2wrk was founded in Oakland, CA to improve access to employment information for people with low literacy and limited access to broadband. Specifically, we wanted to help parolees, who make up a disproportionate percentage of Oakland’s population, find jobs. In addition to low literacy, parolees seeking work are challenged by infrequent Internet connectivity. Typically, their only access is one hour per day at a career center or public library.

Txt2wrk helps parolees and other job seekers compete on a more level playing field by providing

  • text-to-speech delivery of job postings on any mobile phone, including inexpensive models
  • real-time alerts of job postings even when a job seeker is not near a computer
  • the ability to apply for a job and submit a resume using any mobile phone
  • 24-hour access to job postings using any mobile phone
  • access to public and private job information through partnerships with organizations dedicated to helping former felons find jobs

For parolees in particular, txt2wrk provides a receipt that can serve as required proof they are applying for jobs.

Beyond serving parolees, txt2wrk empowers a wide range of populations, including immigrants, people without permanent addresses, and women escaping domestic violence.
