With txt2wrk, job seekers can listen to job postings and submit their resume to employers using any mobile phone.

Once a person signs up with txt2wrk, they can receive text message notifications for jobs as they are posted, access spoken job descriptions, and send their resume to prospective employers. txt2wrk works with any phone that can send and receive text messages and make voice calls.

Originally designed for parolees, txt for work also empowers a wide range of populations. The service significantly reduces barriers for people with low literacy and limited access to broadband technologies, in order to help them compete more effectively in today's job market.

We are currently developing txt2wrk for submission to Apps for Communities by July 11, 2011.
