Txt2wrk provides a free job-matching service to employers willing to hire employees who have traditionally been considered "hard to hire," for example, parolees, people in temporary housing, and people with limited literacy skills and limited access to the Internet.

Who are txt2wrk job seekers?

Txt2wrk job seekers are eager to find work, but their job searches have typically been affected by limited access to the internet or low literacy.

Txt2wrk provides job alerts and spoken job descriptions over any mobile phone, enabling people with limited access to spend more time looking for work, and provides people with low literacy an alternative to existing job portals and help-wanted ads.

How can txt2wrk help you?

Job postings are free! Create an account and immediately post as many job postings as you like.

Access untapped pools of job seekers who are ready to work immediately and are not currently being served by existing job portals and help-wanted ads.

Minimize the time between posting a job and receiving applications. Job seekers are notified immediately of new job postings and can submit their resume instantaneously, all using their mobile phone.
